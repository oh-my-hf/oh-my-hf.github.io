# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

ML engineers, researchers, and local-AI hobbyists who already live on the Hugging Face Hub. They land here from GitHub, Discord, or a share link looking for one thing: a trustworthy download of the unofficial desktop client, plus a clear sense of what it does before they install. They are keyboard-fluent, privacy-sensitive, and allergic to official-looking impostors.

## Product Purpose

This site is the public face of **ohmyhf** (Oh My HuggingFace) — an unofficial, open-source, cross-platform desktop client for the Hugging Face Hub. Success: a visitor understands the product in one scroll, trusts that it is unofficial and local-first, and downloads the right installer for their OS from the latest GitHub Release. The site does not sell accounts, collect analytics, or impersonate Hugging Face.

## Brand Personality

Familiar, dense, and quietly confident — “the Hub, distilled into a desktop instrument.” Cool paper grays, hairline borders, black-pill CTAs, one yellow wink from our own icon. Welcoming without cosplay; technical without terminal cosplay.

## Anti-references

- Hugging Face identity impersonation: no HF logo/wordmark, no 🤗 in our chrome, nothing that implies official status
- Dark-mode-by-default marketing for a light-first product
- SaaS hero-metric strips, purple gradient themes, identical icon+card grids
- Numbered section eyebrows (01 / 02 / 03) as page scaffolding
- Electron-app brochure pages that never show the real UI

## Design Principles

1. **Show the product.** Real screenshots carry the story; mockups are a last resort.
2. **Speak the Hub’s dialect, keep our name.** Cool grays, mono repo names, black CTAs — our icon and disclaimers stay ours.
3. **Light is the default scene.** Match the app’s light chrome; dark is optional, not the brand.
4. **Trust before download.** Unofficial status, privacy, and open source are visible before the CTA.
5. **One job per section.** Hero → proof → features → download → FAQ; no dashboard clutter in the first viewport.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Body text ≥ 4.5:1 on light surfaces. Full keyboard access, visible focus rings, `prefers-reduced-motion` honored. English and 简体中文 as first-class locales; CJK system font fallbacks in the stack.
